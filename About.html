Our History

Roberts International Airport (IATA: ROR, ICAO: GIRR), informally also known as Robertfield, is the international airport of liberia. Located near the town of Harbel, the single runway airport is about 35 miles (56 km) outside of the nations capital Monronvia. The facility with it's 11,000 feet (3,400 m) long runway was an emergency landing site for the United States Space Shuttle program and is the principal airport in the country and one ofonly two with paved runways in the raclon. The airport is named in honor of Joseph Jenkins Roberts. The First President of Liberia.
