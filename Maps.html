Parking & Transport

Parking

A parking is available for travelers. It is located just outside the terminal. Parking fee is LD$ 50 / 30 minutes. The droff off fee in front of the terminal is L$ 50. Please acquire ticket upon entry


Due to increase in the accessing to the airport by commercial trucks for business, the airport management has institute fees for cmmercial trucks (4-10 Lyres) USS25.00 for single access, truck (above 10 tyres) US$50.00 for single access equipmentUS$50.00 for single access.

Taxis

Taxis are available as all flight arrivals in Monrovia International Airport. They are recognizable by their yellow color. They do not have on your destination. Any ride must be negotiated with the driver before or lering the vehicle. However count between US$10 and US$20 for a drive between the airport and downtown Monrovia.
